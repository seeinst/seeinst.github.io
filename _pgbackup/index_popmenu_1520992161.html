About Us

We are a non for profit think tank, with a mission to drive and support positive transformations in Africa's energy industrythrough in depth analysis and thought leadership. We aim to bridge the energy knowledge gap within, and about Africa, by informing on viable opportunities and pragmatic solutions to pressing challenges in Africa's energy industry."
